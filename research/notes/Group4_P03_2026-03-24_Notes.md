<!-- Participant 03 (P03) - Student Success Tutor at William College (Education, 1000+ employees) -->

First days: Joined a brand new college. No tools, trackers, or policies in place; had to build everything from scratch

Onboarding experience: Very confusing initially. The line manager didn't meet with them for 17 days, leaving them clueless about actual job responsibilities (thought they might be teaching)
. Colleagues from other departments saved the day with support

Finding Information: Mostly relies on colleagues and trusts their answers without needing verification
. IT took 2-3 weeks just to sort out logins, which was frustrating because no work could be done
. Uses SharePoint heavily

Missing System Capabilities: Needs super-quick access to students' personal phone numbers or next-of-kin contacts for urgent deadlines when students ignore official emails

AI Tools: Uses ChatGPT to draft delicate emails to students (balancing urgency without applying too much pressure) and for Excel help

Trust: Does not rely on it solely; always cross-checks because AI hallucinates details (e.g., gets the days of the week wrong for dates)

Privacy: Highly aware of privacy; never inputs personal student information

Ideal Fix: Wants future colleagues to have one single platform that gives an overview of the department and clear job responsibilities to prevent early stress
