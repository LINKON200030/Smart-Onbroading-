<!-- Participant 02 (P02) - Accountant at Roundhouse Design (Retail, 51-200 employees) -->

First days: Stressful. Had to learn a new accounting system, figure out deadlines, and even manage working on a very small desk

Onboarding experience: Friendly, but heavily DIY. Mostly had to "figure stuff out ourselves" rather than getting professional, step-by-step training

Finding Information: Had to ask teammates initially, search company servers, and check shared drives
 Major friction: The company rotates data/programs every 3 months, forcing them to relearn where things are

Tool Sprawl: Uses at least 20 different systems every day
Constantly scrolling from one to another. Desperately wants a single home screen for deadlines, cash flow, and client receipts

Collaboration: Frustrated by asking questions about documents and waiting a week for an answer. Wants the ability to attach comments directly to quotes/documents
. Costs time and causes stress

AI Tools: Uses ChatGPT, Copilot, Perplexity for drafting invoices and calculations

Dislikes: "Fake empathy" (e.g., "I understand you") and long, windy answers

Trust: Only trusts AI when it breaks down its reasoning and explains why

Privacy: Very worried about financial data. Would require a mandatory consent form proving data gets deleted after use
