## IA Design for Smart Onboarding

The Information Architecture was designed based on the pain points identified in the first week. The overall goal of this design is to reduce cognitive overload while maintaining easy and quick access to all resources.

Rather than showcasing the whole week's tasks, this system is structured to prioritise required tasks through "Priority Tasks" and "Today's Focus". Supporting elements such as Quick Links and Progress Tracking provide context without overwhelming the user.

The Knowledge Hub is presented as a search-based entry point rather than a full content display, supporting the goal of a single source of truth while maintaining a clean interface. Also, the Knowledge Hub content will be dynamic according to today's focus area to provide easy access to relevant resources.

The AI Assistant is implemented as a floating interaction, allowing users to access help contextually without occupying dashboard space. This aligns with modern UX patterns and reduces visual clutter.

This IA directly addresses user pain points identified in research, including information fragmentation, lack of structure, and cognitive overload.
